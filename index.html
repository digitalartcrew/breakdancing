About Us

BreaksR4Kidz is a youth breakdancing organization based in Honolulu, HI that provides breakdancing classes to youth. Led by Coach Tom Solo, our mission is to empower kids through dance, build confidence, and develop discipline through the art of breaking.

Classes are open to all skill levels and offer a safe, positive environment for learning, expression, and fun.
