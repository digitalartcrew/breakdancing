Who We Are

About Us

BreaksR4Kidz is a youth breakdancing organization based in Honolulu, HI. Led by Coach Tom Solo, our mission is to empower kids through dance, build confidence, and develop discipline through the art of breaking.

Classes are open to all skill levels and offer a safe, positive environment for learning, expression, and fun.

Leadership

Meet the Coach

Tom Solo

Director & Head Coach

Coach Tom Solo is a veteran B-Boy and passionate educator who has dedicated himself to bringing breakdancing to Honolulu's youth. With deep roots in hip hop culture and a lifelong commitment to the art of breaking, Tom founded BreaksR4Kidz to give kids in Hawai'i a space to grow, compete, and express themselves.

Under his leadership, BreaksR4Kidz students have performed at state-level events, earned recognition in local media, and are now training for the 2027 AAU Junior Olympics — representing the spirit of Hawai'i on a national stage.

"Breakdancing isn't just a sport — it's a lifestyle, a culture, and a way for every kid to find their power."

🎯 Breakdancing teaches kids discipline and focus.

🤝 Working together builds a strong sense of community.

🌟 Performing boosts confidence and self-esteem.

💪 A fun way to stay physically active and healthy.

🎨 Encourages artistic expression through movement.

🏆 Introduces youth to hip hop culture and its roots.
